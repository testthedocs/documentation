Communication¶

Slack¶

If you “just” want to hang out or you already in the Slack network of WriteTheDocs, join us in the testthedocs channel !

If you are interested in contributing and/or developing in any way, join us in the TestTheDocs network !

Mailing List¶

Please join our Google Group for discussions about the community, tools and much more.

Newsletter¶

Sign up for our newsletter to stay up to date about the community and tools.

The newsletter is low traffic, you will receive it on a irregular basis.
